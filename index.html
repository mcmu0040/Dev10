Food Truck Description

Truck, Truck, Gray Truck is the premeir truck company serving only the best of Minnesota comfort foods. From hotdish to letefisk, we have all of you comfort food needs. We are available for:

  • Corporate Parties
  • Special Events
  • Lunch
  • Parole Hearings
  • Movie Premieres
  • When it's late and you just want to talk about it

We have over 3 trucks in our fleet (though only the red one works well). We are based out of Hudson, WI for tax purposes.
